MEASUREMENTS DESCRIPTION

34 bearings were measured, each pre-streesed in 2 directions for 4 times. Between measurements
the bearings were removed and reinserted randomly into the rotating mass. There are a total of
272 measurements of length 64000 with sampling frequency of 6400 Hz (measurements length = 10s).
- 10 OK bearings - without applied force
- 4 bearings with applied static force of 1500 N in axial direction
- 4 bearings with applied static force of 1500 N in radial direction
- 4 bearings with applied static force of 2500 N in axial direction
- 4 bearings with applied static force of 2500 N in radial direction
- 4 bearings with applied static force of 4166 N in axial direction
- 4 bearings with applied static force of 3200 N in radial direction

Inside measurement_x.pkl there is a dictionary with 6 keys:
- force: force measurement
- sound_pressure: sound pressure measurement
- fault_present: 0 for "no fault" and 1 for "fault
- fault_type: 0 for "no fault", 1 for "radial" and 2 for "axial"
- fault_severity: 0 for "no fault", 1 for "boundary", 2 for "low" and 3 for "high"
- applied_force: applied force in N
